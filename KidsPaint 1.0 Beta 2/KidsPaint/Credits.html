About

KidsPaint is a fun and easy-to-use drawing application designed specifically for children and families. It encourages creativity by providing a simple interface with a variety of brush tips and stickers, allowing users to express themselves through art.

The app supports multiple input methods, including mouse, trackpad, and game controllers, making it accessible and enjoyable for users of all ages and skill levels.
