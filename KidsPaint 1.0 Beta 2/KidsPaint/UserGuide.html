User Guide

KidsPaint
by Fivethirty Softworks

KidsPaint is a simple, open-source drawing app for kids (and parents!) that focuses on creativity without clutter. There are no documents to manage and no confusing modes — just open the app, paint, and save.

What You Can Do

  • Paint with multiple brush types
    Round, Square, Chisel, Crayon, Spray Paint, and Neon brushes add variety without complexity.
  • Add big, fun stickers
    Star, heart, smile, flower, sun, moon & stars, cloud, lightning bolt, balloons, crown, music note, and paw print.
  • Change colors and sizes easily
    Choose from 10 preset colors or use the full Color Picker. Adjust sizes with a simple slider.
  • Draw on top of photos
    Import PNG, JPG, or TIFF files and paint on top of them without modifying the original file.
  • Use a game controller to paint
    Works with Xbox, PlayStation, MFi-supported, and many Made-for-iPhone/iPad controllers on macOS.
  • Save artwork as PNG
    No special file formats. What you see is exactly what gets saved.

KidsPaint is built for ease of use, with a straightforward interface and a carefully chosen set of features kids enjoy most.

Main Tools (Top Toolbar)

Brush

  • Draw freely on the canvas.
  • Use the Size slider to change line thickness.
  • Use the Color bar to change color.
  • Supports multiple brush tip styles.

Eraser

  • Works like the brush, but erases instead of drawing.
  • Size controls how wide the eraser is.

Stickers

  • Place large, colorful stickers.
  • Choose a sticker from the bottom sticker strip.
  • Click anywhere on the canvas to place it.
  • Stickers can be moved, resized, rotated, and deleted.

Move

  • Pan (drag) the canvas around.
  • Pinch or scroll to zoom in and out.
  • Does not draw or place stickers.

Bottom Control Bar (Kid-Friendly Controls)

The bottom bar updates based on the selected tool.

Color Selection

  • Large color circles for quick picking.
  • Full Color Picker for custom colors.
  • Color changes affect new drawings and new stickers only.

Size Control

  • Brush / Eraser: controls line thickness.
  • Stickers: controls how large new stickers are (intentionally big).

Brush Tip Selection

  • Icon-only tip selector.
  • Available tips:
    • Round
    • Square
    • Spray
    • Chisel
    • Crayon
    • Neon

Sticker Strip

  • Icons only (no text).
  • Tap a sticker to select it.
  • Scroll horizontally to see more stickers.

Working With Stickers

Select a Sticker

  • Click once to select.
  • Handles appear around the sticker.

Move

  • Drag the sticker anywhere on the canvas.

Resize

  • Drag the square handle.

Rotate

  • Drag the round handle.
  • Hold Shift to snap rotation.

Delete

  • Select the sticker.
  • Press Delete or Backspace.

Game Controller Support (macOS)

KidsPaint supports any controller recognized by macOS, including:

  • Xbox Wireless Controllers (Xbox One / Series X|S)
  • PlayStation DualShock 4 and DualSense
  • MFi-supported controllers
  • Many Made-for-iPhone/iPad controllers

Controller Mapping

  • Left Stick – Move the on-screen cursor
  • A / Cross
    • Tap → place a single brush mark
    • Hold + move → continuous drawing
  • D-Pad Left / Right – Cycle colors
  • D-Pad Up / Down – Cycle brush tips
  • Triggers / Bumpers – Adjust brush size or zoom (when available)
  • Menu / Options – Undo / Redo (controller-dependent)
Tip: Click once on the canvas to ensure it has focus if controller input seems inactive.

Mouse and trackpad always work alongside controller input.

Background Images

Open an Image

  • Click Open Image (photo icon).
  • Choose a PNG, JPG, or TIFF file.
  • The image becomes the canvas background.

Remove Background

  • Open the More (•••) menu.
  • Choose Remove Background Image.

Saving Your Artwork

Save as PNG

  • Click Save PNG (download icon).
  • Choose a location and filename. The default filename is KidsPaint MM-DD-YY HH-MM-SS.png. This will prevent accidentally overwriting previously saved files.
  • Your artwork is saved as a standard PNG image.

KidsPaint does not use special document formats, all saved work is standard PNG format.
What you save is exactly what you see.

Clearing the Canvas

  • Click the Trash icon.
  • Confirm when asked.
  • Removes all drawings and stickers.

(The confirmation is intentional to prevent accidents.)

View Controls

Reset View

  • Click Reset View (circular arrow).
  • Zoom and position return to normal.

Zoom

  • Pinch on a trackpad or scroll to zoom.

Pan

  • Use the Move tool to drag the canvas.
