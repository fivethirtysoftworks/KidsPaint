Privacy Policy for KidsPaint by Fivethirty Softworks

Last updated: December 29th, 2025

KidsPaint by Fivethirty Softworks respects your privacy by not touching your data at all.

What data we collect

None.

KidsPaint does not collect, track, store, transmit, sell, or analyze:

  • Personal information
  • Usage data
  • Analytics
  • Location data
  • Identifiers
  • Crash reports tied to you

We don’t want your data. We don’t need it.

How KidsPaint works

All drawings, creations, and interactions happen locally on your device. If you save or export artwork, it stays where you choose to put it.

Third-party services

KidsPaint does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking SDKs, or data brokers.

Children’s privacy

KidsPaint is designed to be safe and simple for kids. Because we collect no data whatsoever, there is nothing to misuse, share, or leak.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, it will only be because the app fundamentally changes how it works. If that ever happens, this policy will be updated clearly and honestly.

We don’t collect your data. We don’t sell your data. We don’t track you. Your stuff stays on your device. Keep your data to yourself — that’s how we like it.
